Wine Networks
Digging into how wines are connected based on similarities

Have you ever tried a wine you really loved and then become hooked on this exact wine, not willing to try something new? Are your dinner friends no longer impressed? Look no further! On this website, extensive analyis has been made in order to provide you with deeper knowledge about wines, their flavours, grapes and similarities – so you can find a wine just as good as your go-to wine.

All analysis is based on data scraped from Vivino.com, the world's largest online wine marketplace, and contains everything from the wine producer and region, to the most used grapes in the wine and good food pairings, to user's ratings and average prices. To keep it semi simple and stick to the most importatnt wines, this website focuses on Italian and French wines.
